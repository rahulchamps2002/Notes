  1. Consider the system of components connected as in the accompanying picture. Components 1 and 2 are connected in parallel so that subsystem works if either 1 or 2 workds; since 3 and 4 are connected in series that subsystem works if both 3 and 4 work. If components work independently of one another and P(component i works) = 0.9 for i = 1,2 and = 0.8 for i = 3,4, calculate {(system works).

    P(system works) = 1 - P(system fails)
    = 1 - P(subsystem 1 fails ∩ subsystem 2 fails)
    = 1 - P(subsystem 1 fails) * P(subsystem 2 fails)
    P(subsystem 1 fails) = P(comp 1 and 2 fail)
    (0.1)2 = 0.01
    P(subsystem 2 fails) = 1 - P(subsystem 2 works)
    = 1 - P(comp 3 and 4 work)
    = 1 - (0.8)2 = 0.36
    P(system works) = 1 - 0.01(0.36) = 1 - 0.0036 = 0.9964
    P(system works) = 0.9964
